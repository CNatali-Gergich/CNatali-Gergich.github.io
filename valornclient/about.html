About Valorn

Lev “valorn987” Sladkov is a gaming content creator and community builder, streaming on Twitch and YouTube. His mission is to bring people together through creativity, teamwork, and fun competition.

When Valorn isn't live, he's planning new stream ideas, editing videos, and looking for ways to involve his community in every project. From ranked grinds and challenge runs to community nights and collabs, Valorn focuses on keeping streams positive, interactive, and welcoming for players of all skill levels.
