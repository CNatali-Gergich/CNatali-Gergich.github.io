About Courageous Needlefish

Courageous Needlefish began with a single idea: make the best hook remover tool an angler could trust. After hours on the water and frustrating catches that took more time to release than to land, we set out to design tools that merged precision, safety, and style.

Today, we create a variety of hook removal tools including pliers, grippers, and other modern tackle-accessories that operate as smoothly as they look. Each tool is crafted for durability, ease of use, and standout performance so you can focus on the catch and not the equipment.
