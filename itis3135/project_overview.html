Project Overview

This project is a web application created for Lev “valorn987” Sladkov, a gaming content creator who streams under the handle valorn987 on both Twitch and YouTube. The purpose of the website is to provide a professional online hub that showcases his gaming highlights, connects fans to his social platforms, and helps him find new players to collaborate and play with. The site will improve visibility of his content and offer a single destination where followers and potential teammates can learn about his gaming journey.

Intended Users

  • Fans and followers who want to learn more about Valorn and his streams.
  • Potential collaborators or teammates looking to play with him.
  • Sponsors or organizations interested in his gaming content and community reach.

Overview of the Website Content

  • Personal information about Lev “valorn987” Sladkov and his gaming background.
  • Embedded highlight videos from Twitch and YouTube.
  • Information about streaming schedule, gaming setup, and favorite titles.
  • Contact and collaboration forms for other players and sponsors.
  • Social media links and contact information.

Wireframe

The wireframe for this project was created using Draw.io. It outlines the layout for the homepage of the Valorn Gaming website, including a top navigation bar, featured stream/video section, introduction text, highlights gallery, and a consistent footer across all pages.

Site Map

The site map below shows the navigation structure of the Valorn Gaming website. The Home page connects to five primary subpages — About, Content, Schedule, Collab / Play With Me, and Contact — each focusing on different aspects of Lev's gaming content and community engagement.

Page Designs

  1. Home (index.html): Introduces the Valorn brand, slogan, and featured video. Includes navigation to all other sections and an embedded highlight video that updates dynamically.
  2. About (about.html): Shares Lev's story, gaming background, and setup. Audience: fans and collaborators.
  3. Content (content.html): Displays embedded clips from Twitch and YouTube with interactive thumbnails and lightbox interactivity. Audience: general visitors.
  4. Schedule (schedule.html): Features an interactive JavaScript calendar showing Lev's upcoming streams, collaborations, and events. Audience: returning viewers and teammates.
  5. Collab / Play With Me (collab.html): Page for players interested in teaming up. Includes a contact form with basic validation.
  6. Contact / Links (contact.html): Contains official social links, Discord tag, and validated contact form for sponsors and fans.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Interactive Video Gallery: JavaScript-driven gallery on the Content page that allows users to expand and view embedded Twitch and YouTube clips dynamically.
  • Interactive Calendar: A jQuery UI-powered datepicker on the Schedule page allows users to click a date to view upcoming streams or events.
  • Form Validation: JavaScript validation on the Collab and Contact pages ensures all required fields are completed before submission. Uses a front-end form integrated with FormSubmit.co to send messages directly to the client's email. No server-side code or database storage is required, keeping the site secure and lightweight.
